#Minecraft Color Codes
In Nucleus, to use these color codes in chat or other server messages, prefix the number or letter with the "&" symbol.

&0 0 Black
&1 1 Dark Blue
&2 2 Dark Green
&3 3 Dark Aqua
&4 4 Dark Red
&5 5 Dark Purple
&6 6 Gold
&7 7 Gray
&8 8 Dark Gray
&9 9 Blue
&a a Green
&b b Aqua
&c c Red
&d d Light Purple
&e e Yellow
&f f White

&r k Magic &kMagic
&l l Bold
&m m Strikethrough
&n n Underline
&o o Italic

&r r Reset
